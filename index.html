Solar Panel

Solar panels in electric vehicles (EVs) provide supplementary power for onboard systems, potentially extending the vehicle's range and reducing grid dependency. Despite challenges like limited surface area, ongoing research aims to enhance efficiency, fostering innovation in design and promoting sustainable transportation. Future prospects suggest an increasing role for solar panels in EVs as technology evolves

Sodium Ion Battery

Sodium-ion batteries are an emerging type of rechargeable battery that uses sodium ions as the charge carriers. Sodium-ion batteries are being explored as an alternative to lithium-ion batteries due to the abundance of sodium and lower production costs. However, challenges such as lower energy density and larger cell sizes compared to lithium-ion batteries are being addressed through ongoing research and development.
